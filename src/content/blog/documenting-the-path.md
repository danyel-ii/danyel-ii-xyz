This site is meant to function as a working record.

The goal is not to present finished expertise. The goal is to keep a clean trail of what was studied, what was built, and what still needs clarification. Writing publicly makes the gaps easier to see.

I want the blog section to remain practical: short notes, references, and decisions that are worth revisiting later.
