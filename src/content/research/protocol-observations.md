Protocols become easier to understand when they are treated as administrative systems rather than abstract diagrams.

What fields exist, which assumptions are encoded, what gets ignored, and where trust is delegated are usually more interesting than memorizing a simplified flow chart. These notes are meant to stay close to that level of observation.
